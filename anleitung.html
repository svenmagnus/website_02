Tangent Club – User Guide

Private 1:1 Video & Lovense Toy Control
Tangent Club is built exclusively for private 1:1 connections – there are no public rooms with multiple viewers. A session always consists of exactly two people (video + optional toy control). Multiple independent 1:1 sessions can run on the platform at the same time.

1. Invitations & Connecting with your Partner

Tangent Club is a closed, private community. Connections are built either by inviting new people to join the platform or by starting a direct session with existing members.

Inviting People to the Platform

Anyone can invite new people to join Tangent Club. You do not need to wait for an administrator to add your friends or partners.

  • Open your account menu in the top right corner (by clicking your profile picture).
  • Click Invite someone to generate an invitation link or send an invite directly.
  • Alternatively, you can go to the Sessions & calendar section and click the Send invitation via email button to invite a guest directly to the platform.

Starting or Scheduling a Session

Once your partner is registered on the platform, you can initiate a session under Setup → Sessions & calendar:

  • Instant Sessions: Select your partner from the Session with dropdown menu, click the New session button, and choose Start instant session.
  • Scheduled Sessions: If you want to plan ahead, select your partner, enter a future date and time under Scheduled start (for later / calendar), and save it. Your partner will be notified automatically.

Going Live (Quick Steps)

  1. Pick your Partner: Go to the Member Pool (right). Find your partner and double-click their card. They will instantly appear in the Partner for this session box (left).
  2. Start the Session: Click Start Instant Session right next to your partner’s card.
  3. Turn on your Camera & Mic: Select your webcam and click Start Camera. Your video feed appears immediately for both of you. Click Start Micro to enable audio.
  4. Partner Joins In: Your partner simply clicks Join Session (the button glows green when you are already live) and turns on their camera/mic.

💡 Optional Lovense Toys: If you want to use them, connect your Lovense toys before starting the session (see details below).

2. Lovense Setup (Before Each Session)

The Lovense Chrome extension links your physical toys to your Tangent Club browser tab.

The Checklist

  1. Lovense Connect (PC/Mac): Start the desktop app, power on your toys, and confirm they show as connected.
  2. Chrome Extension: Click the pink Lovense icon in Chrome. Ensure the extension is On and the cam site Tangent-Club is selected (written exactly like this, with a hyphen and capital T and C).
  3. The Web Platform: Open tangent-club.com, sign in, and open the green Lovense floating menu once on that tab.
  4. Status Check: In the Setup tab of the website, confirm it says Extension ready.

Note: The “Live” label in the Chrome sidebar extension only means the site configuration is active; Extension ready on the webpage is the only indicator that the connection is working. If it fails, click Retry extension connection or reload the page.

Extension Rules (One-Time Setup)

In the Lovense Chrome Extension, go to Tangent-Club → Rules:

  • Basic Levels: Set to OFF. Tangent Club uses its own direct motor controls, not the extension’s automated token ranges.
  • Special Commands: Enable the following patterns with their exact token values (used as internal IDs to trigger the patterns):
    Command Tokens Status
    Earthquake 100 ON
    Fireworks 120 ON
    Wave 160 ON
    Pulse 200 ON
  • Click the pink Save button (top right).

3. Toy Control & Features

  • Local Test (Your Toys): Test your connected toys anytime on your own machine. Sliders commit motor changes on release; presets (Low / Medium / High / Max / Ultra) apply immediately.
  • Remote Control: Once your video and data channels are active, your partner can control your toys via this tab. The toy list is automatically shared.
  • Profile Settings: List your toys under Profile → Lovense toys (owned) so your partner knows what you have (informational only). Click Save profile before leaving.
  • Interface Customization: The Messages panel can be dragged by its title bar and resized from the corner to fit your layout.

4. The “Playbook” & “Play Mode” (Technique Requests)

The Playbook is your personal menu where you define your dynamics, kinks, and preferred interactions. It allows you and your partner to preview preferences and quickly communicate desires during a live stream.

Profile Setup (Your Preferences)

  • Activate Categories: In your My Profile tab, check the boxes under Dynamics & roles, Kink & practices, Intensity & vibe, or Create own menus. Checking a category instantly unlocks its specific action checklist in the playbook section below.
  • Select Actions: Scroll down to My Playbook and check the specific actions you want to explore (e.g., Try something new, Surprise me).
  • Custom Customization: You can build custom grouped menus (e.g., Master → Sub) and add individual custom actions (e.g., Slap ass).
  • Save: Always click the pink Save profile button at the bottom to store your setup.

Pre-Session Preview & Interaction

  • Pre-Session Preview: You can view a partner’s preferences even before a video session begins. Simply double-click their card in the Member Pool to move them into the Partner for this session box. Their enabled items will immediately become visible under the Play Mode tab on the right.
  • During a Live Stream: Once your video session is active, your partner can use this Play Mode tab to click any of your allowed actions.
  • Live Chat Notifications: Clicking an action instantly pushes it as a notification into the Live Chat history so you can react in real time. You can also select a specific Request sound (like a Harp) to play an audio cue whenever a request is sent.
  • Clearing the Selection: Clicking the Clear partner button in the Partner for this session box will instantly reset the screen, clearing both the temporary chat history and the loaded Play Mode features.

5. Advanced: Multi-Streaming & OBS

If you want to use advanced camera features or stream to multiple platforms simultaneously (e.g., Tangent Club and Chaturbate parallel), the standard browser extension will block you. Use one of these two methods:

Option 1: Lovense Stream Master (Recommended)

This is a modified OBS Studio created by Lovense to handle multi-platform toy integration.

  • How it works: Install Stream Master, log in, and pair your toys.
  • Setup: Use the built-in “Multiple Output” function to enter the RTMP server data/keys for Chaturbate and Tangent Club.
  • Advantage: Includes a unified ChatBox to aggregate signals and chats from different platforms.

Option 2: OBS Studio with “Multiple RTMP” Plugin

If you already use standard OBS Studio to feed video into Tangent Club via WHIP/WHEP:

  • Video Setup: Install the free Multiple RTMP outputs plugin for OBS to send your video feed to Chaturbate and Tangent Club simultaneously.
  • Tangent Club Connection: In the Tangent Club Setup tab under broadcast, select OBS Studio (WHIP). Copy the OBS Server (WHIP) URL and Bearer Token into OBS (Settings → Stream → Service: WHIP), then click Start Streaming in OBS. Host clicks Start Camera on the site to pull the feed.
  • Toy Setup: Install the Lovense OBS Toolset. Since the browser extension can only track one active site, toy control will run primarily via Chaturbate (for automated tips). For Tangent Club, triggers must either be mirrored manually via the Lovense Web Dashboard or integrated directly into the backend using the Lovense Developer API.

6. Troubleshooting

  • No Camera/Microphone: Check browser permissions, reload the page, and ensure the correct device is selected in the Setup tab.
  • Cannot Join Session: Ensure the host has completed the New session → Start instant session workflow and is actively live before the partner clicks Join.
  • Toys Do Not Vibrate via Sliders: Use Local Test instead of Send Test Tip (which only tests extension rules). Check the motor hint displayed directly under the toy card.
  • Server Unreachable (Development): Ensure the host is running the required API tunnel (see project technical documentation).
