Community Guidelines: Tangent-Club

Welcome to Tangent-Club. We are a platform dedicated to exclusive 1:1 intimacy. Our community is built on mutual respect, enthusiastic consent, and an equal connection. By using our services, you agree to abide by these principles.

1. Consent & Respect

Intimacy only works on the basis of absolute, enthusiastic consent. We do not tolerate any form of coercion, discrimination, or hateful behavior. As we are not a commercial “mass-market” platform, our focus is on the quality of the interaction and connection on equal terms.

2. Zero Tolerance for Violations

Protection of Minors

Tangent-Club is strictly 18+. Any depiction of minors, age-play, or pretending to be under 18 will result in immediate permanent exclusion.

No Violence or Self-Harm

Content that depicts or simulates physical violence, extreme pain, animal cruelty, or self-harm is strictly prohibited.

Privacy

Tangent-Club is a protected 1:1 space. Doxing, threats, or the distribution of another user’s personal information are strictly forbidden.

3. Focus on the Dyadic Relationship

At Tangent-Club, there is no “viewer-performer” hierarchy. We do not use token systems or commercial performance pressure. Interaction takes place between two people who freely decide on their shared dynamic—whether it is within a BDSM context or through playful remote control via hardware.

4. Genuine Connection over Commerce

As we are not a commercial platform, Tangent-Club is not a place for:

  • Advertising or promotion of third-party sites.
  • The sale of services or escort offers.
  • Gambling or unauthorized commercial activities.

5. Responsibility & Safety

Presence

We expect all participants to be present and engaged. “Unattended streams” or performing while impaired by drugs or alcohol is prohibited.

Originality

Use only content you have created yourself or have explicit permission to share. The use of deepfakes or AI-generated identities is strictly prohibited to protect individual privacy.

Hardware Usage

The integration of Lovense technology serves as a tool for intimacy. Please ensure all devices used meet safety standards and are used responsibly.

Summary

We are creating a space for genuine encounters. If you are unsure whether a certain behavior aligns with our principles, remember: Always ask. Everything we do must be “safe, sane, legal, and consensual.” In the event of violations, we reserve the right to permanently exclude users to ensure the safety of our community.
