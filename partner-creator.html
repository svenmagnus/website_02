Become a Partner / Creator

You are a professional cam model. Build premium sessions on Tangent Club — transparent pricing, escrow payments, and Stripe Connect payouts. Paid work is an incentive; real connection comes first.

How much can I earn?

Premium sessions include transparent pricing, a clear platform commission, and automated payout statements.

You agree rates directly with members. We do not set your prices — you and your partner decide what a session is worth before anyone pays into escrow.

More than paid sessions

Tangent Club is still growing. Paid bookings are one way to earn, but they are not the only focus. The platform is about spending time together — chat, play, and mutual respect. Compensation should support that relationship, not replace it.

We onboard only a limited number of models so competition stays manageable. As the site develops, other lucrative earning models may emerge. One option we are exploring is a share of membership revenue once there is a strong member base.

There are no fixed working hours and no obligation to go online. You choose when and how you take part.

How safe is the platform?

We are adding consent-first session flow, role-based controls, and stronger account/admin security.

How do payouts work?

Models receive 60% of each booked session (platform fee 40%, configurable per model). Guest payments are held in escrow on the platform account until the session is marked complete — then payout is sent to your bank via Stripe Connect.
