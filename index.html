About Tangent-Club

What is Tangent Club?

Tangent Club is a private 1:1 video space—built for professionals who want to move beyond the limitations of public cam sites. This isn’t a place for anonymous crowds, public galleries, or the noise of a tipping room. It is a secure, private bridge for you and your clients. Whether you are hosting an intimate session or a high-end interactive experience, you connect only with those you invite.

True Real-Time Performance

The difference starts with our P2P architecture, which completely eliminates the 3- to 5-second lag typical of public platforms. When you perform, your client sees you instantly. This lack of latency is the foundation for a professional flow where every movement, gesture, and reaction is perfectly synchronized. When a client triggers an interactive toy, the response happens in the exact same moment. This creates an immersive, seamless environment where technology disappears, and only the connection between you and your client remains.

Your Exclusive Professional Space

Tangent Club operates like a private digital studio rather than a public broadcast channel. By removing the public gallery, we have removed the pressure to “perform for the masses.” You are no longer one of hundreds of thumbnails fighting for attention; you are the sole focus of the session. This environment allows you to cultivate a more professional and premium atmosphere, where your expertise as a creator is the main event. It creates a space where clients feel they are in a private, high-value setting, which naturally elevates the quality of your interactions and the time clients are willing to spend with you.

Quality Over Quantity

Because Tangent Club is designed for 1:1 sessions, you can focus on building deeper, more meaningful relationships with your existing clients. This is not about getting as many people into a chat room as possible; it is about providing an elite experience that differentiates you from the rest of the industry. Clients appreciate the exclusivity and the technical stability of our platform, which in turn fosters higher retention and more consistent bookings. You are moving from a “cam-model” model to a “private consultant” level of service.

Privacy and Professionalism

Your business is your own. With our P2P-based foundation, your private sessions remain exactly that—private. Because the data flows directly between you and your client, there is no centralized storage or public footprint. This, combined with our automated Stripe Connect payment system, ensures that the administrative side of your work is completely taken care of. You can walk into a session knowing that the tech is rock-solid and the payment is handled automatically, leaving you free to focus entirely on your work and your clients.

Connection & broadcast

Quick Start: Connecting with your Partner

Getting started is easy! Just follow these simple steps to connect:

  1. Pick your Partner
    Go to the Member Pool on the right. Find your partner and double-click their card. They will instantly appear in the Partner for this session box on the left.
  2. Start the Session
    Click the Start Instant Session button right next to your partner’s card.
  3. Turn on your Camera & Mic
    Select your webcam and click Start Camera. Your video feed will appear immediately for both you and your partner. Do the same for your microphone (Start Micro) to enable audio.
  4. Partner Joins In
    Now, your partner simply turns on their camera and microphone on their end, and you are ready to go!

Optional: Lovense Toys

  • Connect your Toys — If you want to use them, connect your Lovense toys now so they are ready for the session.
